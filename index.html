About Me

I'm a B.Tech CSE student with a specialization in Cybersecurity. I started coding at 18 because I wanted to build my own video games, and that spark grew into a passion for web development — turning designs into living, interactive interfaces.

Along the way I discovered cybersecurity, and something clicked. The same curiosity that made me want to build things made me want to understand how they break — and how to defend them. Now I'm pursuing both: crafting clean frontends on one side, learning about networks, vulnerabilities, and ethical hacking on the other.

Every project teaches me something new — whether it's a CSS grid trick or understanding how a protocol can be exploited. I believe the best developers are also the best security thinkers.
